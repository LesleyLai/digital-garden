# Linearity of Derivative

[[./derivative|Differentiation]] is an [[./linear transformation|linear operator]] of functions:
$$
\begin{aligned}
\left[D(f + g)\right] &= [Df] + [Dg] \\
[D(cf)] &= c[Df]
\end{aligned}
$$

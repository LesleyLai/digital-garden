Let $x$ and $y$ be [[./real number|real numbers]]. We say $x > y$ if $x - y$ is [[./positivity of real numbers|positive]], $x < y$ if $x - y$ is negative, and $x = y$ if $x - y$ is 0.

We can use the similar method to define $\ge$ and $\le$.

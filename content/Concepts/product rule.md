# Product Rule

### In Multi-dimension

The product rule is defined in term of [[./dot product|dot product]] in multi-dimension: $(\mathbf{u} \cdot \mathbf{v})' = \mathbf{u}' \cdot \mathbf{v} + \mathbf{u} \cdot \mathbf{v}'$

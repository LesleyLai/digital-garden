The $L_\infty$ norm of a vector $x$ measures its largest components.
$$
\begin{equation*}
\| \mathbf{x} \|_\infty = \max( \vert x_0 \vert, \ldots , \vert
x_{m-1} ) = \max_{i=0}^{m-1} \vert x_i \vert.
\end{equation*}
$$

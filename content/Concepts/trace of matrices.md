# Trace
The **trace** $\operatorname{Tr}(A)$ of a $n \times n$ [[square matrix|square matrix]] $A$ is the sum of all its diagonal elements:
$$
\operatorname{Tr}(A) = \sum_{i=1}^n a_{ii}
$$

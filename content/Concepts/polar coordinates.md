Polar to Cartesian coordinates
$$
\begin{align}
x &= r\cos{\theta} \\
y &= r\sin{\theta}
\end{align}
$$

Cartesian to Polar coordinates
$$
\begin{align}
r &= \sqrt{ x^2 + y^2 } \\
\theta &= \text{atan2}(y, x)
\end{align}
$$

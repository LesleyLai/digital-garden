$a^x = y \Leftrightarrow \log_{a}y = x$

For $f(x) = \log_{b} x$, $b > 0$, $b \ne 1$, $x > 0$

## Identities
- Identity $\log_{b} b = 1$
- zero $\log_{b} 1 = 0$
- Inverse
  - $\log_{b} b^x = x$
  - $b^{\log_{b}x} = x$
- $\log_{b} (x \ y) = \log_{b}x + \log_{b} y$
- $\log_{b} (x / y) = \log_{b}x - \log_{b} y$
- $\log_{b} N^p = p \log_{b} N$
- change of base $\log_{a} x = \frac{\log_b x}{\log_b a}$

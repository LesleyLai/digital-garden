# $L_{p}$ Norm of a Vector
$$
\begin{equation*}
\| x \|_p = \sqrt[p]{\vert x_0 \vert^p + \cdots + \vert
x_{m-1} \vert^p} = \left( \sum_{i=0}^{m-1} \vert x_i 
\vert^p \right)^{1/p}.
\end{equation*}
$$

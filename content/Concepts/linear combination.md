# Linear Combination

A **linear combination** of vectors $\mathbf{v}_1, \cdots, \mathbf{v}_n$ is an expression of the form
$$
  a_1 \mathbf{v}_1 \cdots a_n \mathbf{v}_n
$$
where $a_1, \cdots, a_n$ are scalars.

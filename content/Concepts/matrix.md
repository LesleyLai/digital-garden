# Matrix
 size: $\underbrace{m}_\text{rows} \times \underbrace{n}_\text{columns}$

> [!example]+
> $$
> \begin{aligned}
> & 3 \times 2: \quad &B=\left(\begin{array}{ll}
> a & b \\
> c & d \\
> e & f
> \end{array}\right) \\
> & 2 \times 3: &C=\left(\begin{array}{lll}
> a & b & c \\
> d & e & f
> \end{array}\right)
> \end{aligned}
> $$

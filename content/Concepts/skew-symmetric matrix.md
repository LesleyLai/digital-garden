# Skew-Symmetric Matrix
$A$ is a **skew symmetric matrix** if $A^T = -A$.

Example:
$$
\left[\begin{array}{ccc}
0 & b & c \\
-b & 0 & e \\
-c & -e & 0
\end{array}\right]
$$

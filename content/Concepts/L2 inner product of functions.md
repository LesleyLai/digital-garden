Just like we have [[./L2 norm#L2 Norm of Functions|L2 norm of functions]], we also have **L2 inner product of functions**.

For two real functions $f$ and $g$,
$$
\langle f,\, g \rangle_{L^2} := \int_0^1 f(x) g(x) dx
$$

The **Heaviside step function** or **unit step function** is defined as the following:
$$
u(t) = \begin{cases}
1 & t \ge 0 \\
0 & t < 0
\end{cases}
$$

The derivative of the step function is the [[./Dirac delta|delta function]]:
$$
\frac{d}{dt}u(t) = \delta(t)
$$
